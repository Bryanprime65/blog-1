His father and mother were forced to send Goku to another planet called Earth. On Earth Goku was found by a old man named Grandpa Gohan, he raised Goku and even helped him after a dangerous fall ending up hitting his head and forgetting his evil intentions. He grew up meeting freinds and searching for the dragonballs and having adventures. He then encounters evil and powerful foes that threatend the destruction of the planet and the universe and its up to goku to stop them.
